Our Story

Made for the intentional.

Ethos was born from a simple frustration: the modern approach to finding a life partner is broken. Swiping reduces people to photos. Algorithms optimise for engagement, not compatibility. And the people who are most serious about finding their person are often the least well-served.

We believe that what makes two people truly compatible isn't how they look in a photo — it's how they see the world. Their values, their life goals, the way they approach relationships. The things you only discover after months of getting to know someone, condensed into a system that surfaces them from the start.

How Ethos is different

Values first. Every Ethos member completes a compatibility questionnaire that explores core values, life goals, lifestyle preferences, and relationship approach. Your compatibility scores with other members are based on genuine alignment — not demographics, not swiping patterns.

Matchmaking, not swiping. Ethos doesn't give you an infinite feed to scroll through. We show you a curated set of compatible people and encourage real conversations. Quality over quantity, every time.

Built for commitment. Ethos is designed for people who know what they want. Whether you're ready for marriage or looking for a serious, long-term relationship, this is a space for people who are intentional about their future.

Our commitment

We respect your time, your data, and your trust. We don't sell your information. We don't show you ads. We don't use dark patterns to keep you hooked. We succeed when you find your person and leave — and we think that's exactly how it should be.

Ethos is built and operated by Ethos Labs Pty Ltd, based in Sydney, Australia.
